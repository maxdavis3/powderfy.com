How Powderfy Works

01

Subscription Powder Delivery

Set your usage rate and reorder point — Powderfy ships automatically before you run out. Monthly, bi-weekly, and weekly frequency options. Adjust quantities at any time through your account dashboard.

02

On-Demand Rapid Supply

One-off powder orders for urgent production needs. 48-hour standard delivery, 24-hour rush option. All six core alloys in stock: Ti6Al4V, 316L, 17-4 PH, AlSi10Mg, Inconel 718, CoCr.

03

Powder Quality Assurance

Every lot tested: particle size distribution (D10/D50/D90), apparent density, Hall flow rate, and chemical composition. Certificate of conformance and full traceability supplied. Returns accepted for out-of-spec material.
